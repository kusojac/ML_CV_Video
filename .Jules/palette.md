## 2024-05-18 - [Empty States and Tooltips in Flutter]
**Learning:** In Flutter, adding tooltips to `IconButton`s is crucial as it automatically maps to an accessibility label for screen readers. Furthermore, providing a structured empty state with an icon and clear guidance significantly enhances user understanding when no content is present.
**Action:** Always include `tooltip` properties on icon-only buttons in Flutter, and design rich empty states instead of plain text messages.
